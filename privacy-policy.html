Privacy Summary: The MAWI LATIN TO CYRILLIC extension and website do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All text processing is performed locally on your device.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

The MAWI LATIN TO CYRILLIC extension and website are designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Text content that you input or convert
  • Browsing history or website data
  • Location data
  • Device information
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Usage analytics or statistics

2. How the Extension Works

The MAWI LATIN TO CYRILLIC extension operates entirely on your local device:

  • Text Processing: All Latin to Cyrillic conversion happens locally in your browser
  • Cyrillic Detection: Character detection and highlighting occurs on your device only
  • Settings Storage: Extension preferences are stored locally using Chrome's storage API
  • No Network Communication: The extension does not send any data to external servers

3. Website Functionality

The MAWI LATIN TO CYRILLIC website (https://mawi-officiel.github.io/MAWI_LATIN_TO_CYRILLIC/) operates with the same privacy principles:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No cookies are set by our website
